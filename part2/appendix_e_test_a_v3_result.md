## [TEST A] Simple Question to V3 (Base RAG)

### Question

I am in charge of replacing the filter for the SP-X100. What should I do if the filter is stuck and cannot be removed?

### Answer

The provided materials do not contain any specific troubleshooting instructions for when the **SP-X100** filter is stuck and cannot be removed. Regarding the replacement procedure for the SP-X100, it is only instructed to "turn the used filter element counterclockwise to remove it, and then simply pull it out by hand."

For your reference, the replacement procedure for another model, the **SP-X200**, states that if the filter is stuck, you should "use the dedicated Ceramic Spanner (SP-TOOL-01) to loosen it carefully." However, this spanner is not included in the list of required tools for the SP-X100, and it cannot be confirmed from the materials whether it can be used for the SP-X100 as well.

(*Please note that this is advice from outside the provided materials, but forcing it off may cause damage to components or lead to injury. Therefore, we recommend checking with the manufacturer's support desk or consulting other manuals.)
